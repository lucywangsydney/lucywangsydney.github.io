About Me.

Hi! I'm Lucy, a Computer Science student studying at UNSW. Growing up, I have always been fascinated by the power of technology and how it constantly changes the world around us. I love solving problems and learning new things, so being able to code gives me the opportunity and freedom to come up with creative ways to tackle challenges.

Within university, I have played an active role in student societies since day one. I believe joining societies is the perfect way to meet interesting people and to develop a strong network of like-minded peers.

During my free time, I enjoy cooking, drawing, and planning my next travel destination.

Feel free to reach out to me at: lucywangsydney@gmail.com.

Intern | Enterprise Applications

Deloitte - Summer 2020

I am currently a summer intern at Deloitte in the Enterprise Applications Consulting Team. I will update this space at the end of my internship.








Intern | Supply Chain - 2019

Beiersdorf - 2019

I was the third intern to work with the Sydney Supply Chain Team. During my four months with the business, I implemented initiatives within Customer Service and Supply & Demand Planning, and was given the opportunity to lead a logistical and cost efficiency project, which resulted in major cost savings. Intern | Technology Risk - 2019

PwC - 2019

I completed a six month internship at PwC as part of the UNSW Co-op Program. This was my first corporate internship experience and I worked to provide I.T. controls assurance over Financial Services and Wealth Management. PwC has a very supportive Intern and Graduate community and I will always be grateful for my inspiring colleagues and the guidance they have provided me.
